Murder of Neha Hiremath:
Neha Hiremath, a 23-year-old student and daughter of a Congress councillor,
was tragically stabbed to death by her classmate, Fayaz Khondunaik,
on the campus of BVB College in Hubballi, Karnataka, on April 18, 2024.
The incident sparked widespread protests and led to calls for a thorough investigation.
As of February 2025, her family has requested a Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI)
probe due to concerns over the progress of the case.
